About me

Hello! My name is Fei Gao (高飞). I am currently a lecturer at School of Aeronautics, Shandong Jiaotong University. Prior to that, I graduated from Northwestern Polytechnical University with a Ph.D. degree in Aircraft Design in 2021. My research mainly focuses on the fields of decision-making and evaluation, which includes evidence theory, belief rule-based system, fuzzy logic and their applications in these problems.

When I am not working, I love to cook, eat and watch movies. Barbecue is my favorite, and I firmly believe that Gone Girl is the best romantic movie of all time.
