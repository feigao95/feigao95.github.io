Research Interest

My research focuses on the field of decision making and evaluation, which includes the application of methods such as evidence theory, belief rule-based system, and fuzzy logic into these problems. Furthermore, I've also conducted researches on decision-making methods such as belief rule-based system and evidential reasoning. Currently, I focus on the following topics:

  • Optimization and activation of belief rule-based system
  • Application of decision-making methods in evaluation
  • Weapon system evaluation and selection
  • Human reliability analysis
