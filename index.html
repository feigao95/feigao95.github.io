Biography

Dr. Fei Gao received the B.E. degree in Detection, Guidance, and Control Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Honors College), Xi'an, China in 2016 and the Ph.D. degree in aircraft design from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China in 2021. Since 2022, he has been a lecturer at School of Aeronautics, Shandong Jiaotong University. His main research interests include decision-making and weapon system-of-systems analysis. He has published more than 20 papers on Reliability Engineering & System Safety, Expert Systems with Applications, Applied Soft Computing and other journals. He serves as a reviewer for journals including IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems, IEEE Transactions on Reliability, Reliability Engineering & System Safety, Expert Systems with Applications, and many others.
